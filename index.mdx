HCIMS AI is an AI-powered hotel complaint management platform that transforms raw guest feedback into structured, trackable department tickets. Upload a CSV of reviews, and the system automatically distinguishes complaints from appreciations, assigns priority, routes each ticket to the correct department, and alerts your team — all without manual triage.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload guest reviews">
    Sign in as an admin and upload a CSV file containing guest reviews from the **Upload** page. The backend classifies each row as a complaint or appreciation instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tickets are routed automatically">
    The ML pipeline assigns each complaint a department (Housekeeping, Food & Beverage, Engineering, IT Support, or Front Office) and a priority level (High, Medium, or Low).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff resolve tickets with proof">
    Department staff sign in to their dashboard, view assigned tickets, and upload a photo as proof of resolution. The Nonescape AI model verifies that the proof image is genuine — not AI-generated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Admins review analytics">
    Track resolution rates, department health, recurring issues, and overdue tickets through the analytics dashboard with date range filters and dark mode support.
  </Step>
</Steps>
